THE STORY
In the history of painting, Jean-Honoré Fragonard's The Swing (1767) is unmatched in its frivolity and gaiety over the Top romance. At the center of the work, a young woman, suspended in a moment of being, floating above the ground on a crimson-cushioned swing. She flings her kitten-heeled shoe toward a sculpted putto below the swing.
